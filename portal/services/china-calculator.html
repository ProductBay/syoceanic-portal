Frequently Asked Questions

What items can I ship?

Most consumer goods. Special handling needed for restricted cargo like lithium, chemicals, etc.

How are costs calculated?

By comparing actual weight vs volumetric weight (L×W×H / 5000). The higher value is the chargeable weight.

How long does shipping take?

Air: 7–14 days. Sea: 4–8 weeks. Express available depending on supplier.
